Privacy Policy

Last updated: 05.03.2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the application "VIA" (the “App”) handles user information.

By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. General Information

We respect your privacy. The App is designed to work fully offline and processes all user data locally on your device.

We do not collect, store, or transmit personal data to external servers.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

The App does not collect any of the following:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Contacts
  • Precise location data
  • Photos or media files
  • Usage analytics
  • Tracking identifiers

3. Local Data Storage

All charts, settings, and user-created content are stored exclusively on your device.

This data is never uploaded or shared with us or any third party.

You can delete all data at any time by removing the App from your device.

4. Subscriptions and Payments

The App may offer paid subscriptions. All payments are processed by Apple App Store.

We do not collect or have access to any payment or billing information.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking tools.

The only third-party service involved is Apple App Store for payment processing.

6. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on your device’s protection mechanisms (such as passcode or Face ID).

7. User Rights

Because we do not collect or store personal data, we do not process user data requests.

You can manage or delete all data directly on your device by uninstalling the App.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
